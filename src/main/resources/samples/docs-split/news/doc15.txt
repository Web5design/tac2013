<HEADLINE>
Obama to speak on GM bankruptcy: White House
</HEADLINE>
<DATELINE>
Washington, May 31, 2009 (AFP)
</DATELINE>
<TEXT>
<P>
President Barack Obama will discuss General Motors' expected bankruptcy filing,
in remarks planned for 11:55 am (1555 GMT) Monday, the White House announced.
</P>
<P>
In a statement Sunday, the White House said that Obama would deliver remarks on
the roiling US auto industry after meeting Monday with his senior advisors.
</P>
<P>
Obama's comments are to come shortly after the auto giant officially files
bankruptcy paperwork, which US media reported will likely happen at around 8:00
am (1200 GMT) Monday.
</P>
<P>
Obama was later Monday to meet with his National Economic Council staff, the
White House statement said.
</P>
